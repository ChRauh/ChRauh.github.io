This page assembles datasets, code, and analytical tools I have developed through my research.

If you have questions or build on these resources, I’d be happy to hear from you!

     

General purpose data and tools

  • ParlLawSpeech - Linked full-text corpora of 43,582 bills, 28,124 laws, and 3,092,431 plenary speeches from eight European parliaments - Schwalbach / Hetzer / Proksch / Rauh / Sebők

  • ParlSpeech V2 - Full-text corpora of 6.3 million parliamentary speeches in the key legislative chambers of nine representative democracies - Rauh / Schwalbach

  • ParlSpeech - Full-text corpora of 3.9 million plenary speeches in seven European states - Rauh / De Wilde / Schwalbach

  • EU Commissioner speeches - Full-text of corpus of all 13,618 speeches between 1985 and 2020

  • EU Commission press releases - Full-text corpus of all 44,978 press releases published between January 17, 1985, and January 8, 2021

  • Sentiment dicitionary for German political language

  • PastTwitter - R functions to scrape and plot Twitter account info for past points in time from web.archive.org snapshots

  • ShinyApps for human coding of text snippets

  • ShinyApps for descriptive analysis and sharing of text corpora
